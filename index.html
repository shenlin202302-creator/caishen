What Makes a Fu Talisman "Real"?

In Taoist tradition, a Fu (符) is not just a drawing. It's a vessel for energy.

When a Taoist master creates a Fu, they: purify themselves through fasting and meditation, summon the deities—in our case, Cai Shen and the Five Road Gods of Wealth, channel their own Qi into the brush as they write, and seal the energy with a sacred stamp.

The result is a "concentrated knot of Qi"—a portable energy field calibrated to attract specific outcomes. A Fu for wealth doesn't magically create money. It adjusts the energy around you, making your environment more receptive to opportunities.

Our digital talismans work the same way—because intention, not paper, is the true vessel.
