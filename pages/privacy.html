Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Lucky God. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website.

Data We Collect

We only store minimal data locally in your browser using localStorage:

  • Visitor count (anonymous)
  • Number of fortune draws
  • Number of shares
  • Donation status (to keep unlocked content available)

This data is stored only on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services on our website:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google may use cookies to personalize ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your Google account settings.
  • PayPal: For processing voluntary donations. PayPal's privacy policy applies to your payment information.
  • Stripe: For processing credit card donations. Stripe's privacy policy applies to your payment information.
  • Google Fonts: For loading fonts. Google may log your IP address when loading fonts.

Cookies

Third-party services (Google AdSense, etc.) may use cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy and as required for the operation of the service.

Your Rights

Since we do not store any personal information on our servers, there is no personal data for you to access, modify, or delete. You can clear your browser's localStorage to remove any locally stored data from this website.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for general audiences and is not targeted at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the website.
